Welcome to Hotel Indrayani

Your Dependable Dining Stop on Talegaon-Chakan Road

Conveniently situated right on the Talegaon-Chakan bypass in Induri, we are a perfect stop for highway travelers and local families looking for fresh, hygienic, and authentic pure vegetarian food.

At Hotel Indrayani, we believe in serving fresh Indian comfort flavors that build trust and keep you coming back. With our spacious seating layouts, friendly service staff, and plenty of parking space, you can take a relaxed break and enjoy your meal peacefully.

High Hygiene Standards

Our kitchen is deep-cleaned daily to maintain absolute purity and sanitization.

Highway Convenience

Quick access right off the bypass. Easy parking makes it simple to stretch your legs and dine.
